~ The Crystal Hunt ~

How to play?
Fight off against waves of monsters. Destroy the Crystals to weaken the monsters and prevent them from spawning. Increase your strength as you go, and see how long you can last.

Control
[UP ARROW] Jump, [DOWN ARROW] Dodge
[LEFT ARROW] Move left, [RIGHT ARROW] Move right
[A] Basic attack, [S] Ability 1
[D] Ability 2, [SPACE] Ultimate Ability

Abilities
Abilities requires energy to use. Once you have enough energy, the ability icon will light up, indicating that it is available.
